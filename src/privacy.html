The Tidal Wave Sweater Generator uses a connection to your Facebook account only to publish your custom sweater images you want to your Facebook timeline, when you click the "Share image on Facebook" button. Your images will be posted with the caption you type in the text box above this button.

No personal data is being collected by this application.

Please be sure that your sweater images are being successfully posted to your Facebook timeline, in case of any errors or glitches. The author of this application will not be responsible for any lost pictures.

Enjoy!
